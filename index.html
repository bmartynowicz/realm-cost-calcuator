Blend your telemetry volumes with vendor egress tables and Realm's optimized routing to quickly spot cost deltas.

Realm Cost Calculator FAQ

Answers to common questions from security, data, and finance teams evaluating Realm.

How accurate are the Realm Cost Calculator estimates?

The tool combines publicly available egress pricing, published SIEM ingestion tiers, and Realm's own platform rates. Estimates are intentionally conservative and serve as a planning baseline before we tailor a proposal to your retention and regional needs.

Which data sources and SIEM destinations can I model?

You can choose from 50+ enterprise-grade sources and the top 20 SIEM or observability platforms. This includes providers such as AWS CloudTrail, CrowdStrike, Okta, Palo Alto Networks, Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, and Sumo Logic. New integrations are added frequently.

What happens after I find a scenario that works?

Export the summary for your stakeholders and connect with the Realm team. We will validate assumptions, factor in negotiated discounts, and map a migration plan that keeps compliance and retention requirements intact.

These estimates are for planning purposes only. Contact the Realm team to tailor pricing to your specific workloads and data retention policies.
